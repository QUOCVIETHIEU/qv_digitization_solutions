Overall Flowchart

Inbound, production, and return flow

Flow tổng hợp từ supplier đến production line, syrup area và quay lại warehouse.

Stage 1

Inbound to Warehouse

  • Materials from supplier.
  • QC check.
  • RPM warehouse receiving and inventory visibility.
Stage 2

Outbound to Production

  • Deliver PM to each line and input PM to machine.
  • Deliver RM to syrup area and input RM to mixing tank.
  • Connect with barcode syrup system and batching preparation phase.
Stage 3

Return Back to Warehouse

  • Semi-finished goods production area context.
  • Return remaining materials after production.
  • Support material reconciliation and traceable stock movement.

QRCode System for Packaging

So sánh hai loại label chính và tác động tới quá trình vận hành packaging.

Packaging Label Strategy

Label printed from QRCode System

Internal Label

Easily create and print new label on QRCode System.

Advantages

  • Inherits many existing label formats available on the market.
  • Can reuse transfer labels.
  • Easy to trace and audit.
  • Reduce paper consumption and save time.

Disadvantages

  • Labels can be affected by environmental conditions such as tearing or wet surfaces.
  • Still depends on human handling in labeling and label management.
Packaging Label Strategy

Label from Supplier

Supplier Label

Able to reuse labels directly from suppliers.

Benefits

  • Able to reuse labels with code printed directly from suppliers.
  • Save internal printing resources and materials.
  • Speed up the receiving process at warehouse.
  • Reduce repetitive manual work for packaging operations.

Stakeholder Impact

Benefits for Stakeholders

The rollout creates direct value for both suppliers and manufacturers, with different operational gains on each side.

Suppliers

  • Save delivery time.
  • Paperless process.
  • Reduce PO No. printing mistakes.

Manufacturer

  • Productivity enhancement.
  • Purchase order remaining tracking.
  • Wrong label reduction, recovery packaging management and 5-plant standardization.

Benefits Timeline

2024
  • Apply with 100% suppliers bottle, can, box.
  • Reduce 60% of PM labels creating and scanning in goods receipt process.
  • Reduce 60% of paper for PM.
2025
  • Apply with all suppliers of packaging materials.
  • Reduce 90% of PM label creation, scanning and paper usage.

Opportunities

Những hướng mở rộng tiếp theo giúp phần supplier label trở nên thực tế hơn trong vận hành hàng ngày.
